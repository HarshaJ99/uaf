Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization, projects, and how you can get involved.

Common Questions

What is the United Assistance Foundation?

The United Assistance Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to sustainable community development across Sri Lanka. We focus on education, healthcare, infrastructure, and women's empowerment initiatives.

How are donations used?

100% of your donation goes directly to our projects. We operate with minimal administrative costs and do not pay salaries to our leadership team. All funds are carefully allocated to maximize community impact.

Can I volunteer with your organization?

Yes! We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. Whether you're interested in fieldwork, administration, or specialized expertise, we have opportunities for you to contribute. Please contact us through our website.

How can I stay updated on your projects?

You can subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or check our News & Updates page regularly. We also send annual reports to our major donors detailing our achievements and impact.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to the United Assistance Foundation are tax-deductible in Sri Lanka. We provide official receipts for all contributions that can be used for tax purposes.

How can I partner with your organization?

We welcome partnerships with corporations, government agencies, and other NGOs. Partnership opportunities include funding specific projects, providing in-kind donations, or collaborating on joint initiatives. Contact us to discuss partnership options.
